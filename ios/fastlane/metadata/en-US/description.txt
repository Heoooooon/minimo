Oomool is an all-in-one aquarium management platform for pet fish enthusiasts.

From aquarium setup to species tracking, water quality records, and schedule reminders — we help you care for your aquatic companions systematically.

Key Features:

◆ Aquarium Management
• Manage multiple aquariums at a glance
• Record equipment and tank specifications
• Photo gallery to document growth

◆ Species Management
• Search 900+ species catalog
• Record detailed info per individual (adoption date, source, health status)
• Species memo function

◆ Records & Schedules
• Track water quality, water changes, feeding, and more
• Calendar-based record management
• Recurring schedules with push notifications

◆ Community
• Connect with fellow enthusiasts via Q&A boards
• Share posts and photos
• Follow, like, and bookmark features
• Real-time notifications

◆ Data Management
• Automatic/manual data backup
• CSV export support
